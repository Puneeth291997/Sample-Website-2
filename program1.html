Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program conducted online or offline?

This program is conducted online for a duration of 6 months with the help of multiple live instructor-led training sessions.

Do I need to pay anything for executive alumni status?

Executive Alumni Status will be awarded to the participants on successful completion of the program and one time alumni fee payment of ₹5000. This amount is not included in the course fee. However, this is optional.

What is the admissions process?

After you fill in your basic details for the program, our course advisor will speak to you and based on discussion with them your application will be screened. If your application is shortlisted, you will need to fill detailed application form and attend a telephonic interview, which will be conducted by a subject matter expert. Based on your profile and interview, if you are selected, you will receive an admission offer letter.

What is the duration of this program?

As part of this program, you will undergo 7 months of live classes and 1 month will be provided for project completion.

What if I miss the live classes?

If, by any circumstance, if you’ve missed the live class, you will be given with a recording of the class you missed within the next 12 hours. Also, if you need any support, you will have access to the 24*7 technical support for any sort of query resolution and help.

How much time do I have to commit to this Program?

To complete this program, you will have to spare around 6 hours a week to learn. Classes will be held over weekends (Sat/ Sun) and each session will of 3 hrs.

What types of projects will I be working on?

To ensure you make the most of this program, you will be given industry level projects to work on. This is done to make sure that you can get a concrete understanding of what you’ve learnt.

What sort of placement assistance will I be eligible to?

Upon completion of this program, you will be eligible to first prepare using mock interviews and resume preparation followed by min. 3 exclusive interviews with 500+ hiring partners across the globe.

How is the certificate awarded?

Upon completion of all of the requirements of the program, you will be awarded with a joint certificate from SPJIMR and Intellipaat.
